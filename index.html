I am associate professor at Zhengzhou University of Light Industry, Zhen Zhou, China, where I work on TFT, stress sensor, and Logic circuit, etc. Before that, I did my PhD at school of Deparement Of Adnanced Organic Materials Engineering, Yeungnam University, where I was advised by Prof. Se Hyun Kim.
